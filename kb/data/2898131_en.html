NooBaa's bucket allows you to set up bucket quota, for a better data management.

Please note that the calculation is periodical and in low quota size, may not enforce as expected, due to multiple connections or multipart upload. 

To setup bucket quota, navigate to the buckets window

- Select the desired bucket from the list
- Click on the button "Edit Bucket Quota" under the "Data Placement" tab



- A new dialog will let you set the quota for that bucket
