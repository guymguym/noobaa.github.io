NooBaa licensing is very simple. 

Community version

The community version is a fully functional version, limited only by size - and free up to 20TB. 


Enterprise version

Tne enterprise version is different from the community version only in the support level and the scale, which is multi PB scale. 

Payment is always by used capacity and can be monthly subscription or yearly subscription.
